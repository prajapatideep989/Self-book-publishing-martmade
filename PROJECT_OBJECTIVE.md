# Self Book Publishing Mart - Project Objective

## 🎯 Main Objective

The main objective of the **Self Book Publishing Mart** project is to provide a simple, reliable, and user-friendly online platform where anyone can publish their own books and share their thoughts, ideas, and creativity with the world.

---

## 📋 System Aims

The system aims to achieve the following key objectives:

### 1. **Simplify the Book Publishing Process**
- Provide an easy-to-use web application that removes technical barriers to publishing
- Streamline the workflow from book creation to publication
- Eliminate the need for traditional publishing intermediaries
- Enable authors to maintain full creative control over their work

### 2. **User Empowerment**
- Allow users to create, upload, and manage their books independently
- Provide intuitive tools that require no technical expertise
- Enable authors to set their own pricing and manage inventory
- Give publishers complete control over their published content

### 3. **Robust Data Management**
- Maintain a structured database for storing and organizing book details
- Securely manage user profiles and authentication
- Track orders, transactions, and earnings efficiently
- Ensure data integrity and reliability across all operations

### 4. **Global Accessibility**
- Enable readers to explore and engage with published books anytime, anywhere
- Provide 24/7 availability through cloud-based hosting
- Support multiple categories and genres for diverse content
- Facilitate easy discovery through advanced search and filtering

### 5. **Promote Creativity and Knowledge-Sharing**
- Foster a community of writers and readers
- Encourage self-expression through digital publishing
- Enable knowledge-sharing across geographical boundaries
- Support diverse voices and perspectives in literature

---

## 🎨 Core Features

### For Authors/Publishers:
- **Book Upload & Management**: Upload books with cover images, descriptions, pricing, and quantity
- **Earnings Dashboard**: Track sales and revenue (90% revenue share)
- **Approval Workflow**: Submit books for quality review by administrators
- **Profile Management**: Maintain author profiles and manage published works

### For Readers/Buyers:
- **Book Discovery**: Browse and search books by title, author, or category
- **Shopping Cart**: Add multiple books and complete secure purchases
- **Order Tracking**: View purchase history and order details
- **Secure Payments**: Integrated payment gateway for safe transactions

### For Administrators:
- **Content Moderation**: Review and approve submitted books for quality control
- **Platform Management**: Monitor users, books, orders, and platform metrics
- **Revenue Tracking**: Track platform earnings and commission (10% platform fee)
- **User Management**: Oversee the community and ensure platform integrity

## 🛠️ Technical Approach

The platform is built using:
- **Backend**: PHP with MySQL database for robust data management
- **Frontend**: Modern, responsive HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript interface
- **Payment Integration**: Razorpay for secure transaction processing
- **Design Philosophy**: User-centric, intuitive, and accessible
- **Architecture**: WAMP stack for reliable performance

---

## 📈 Success Metrics

The project's success will be measured by:

2. Volume of books available on the platform
3. Reader engagement and purchase conversions
